Tying Of Kyle

Featured

This is by far the most complicated game of them all, with waves of enemies and leveling up and the like. The name is a rough play on the game "The Binding of Isaac" as we originally intended to make multiple rooms. The enemy AI in this game is very advanced and better than most triple A titles.

****NON SMARTBOARD****

Rain

This started the journey to create all of these. It started with a rain animation and then evolved into a simplistic game of filling the bucket. The bucket changes color once it is full and provides a wonderful experience.

Color Triangle Match ****NON SMARTBOARD****

This started with a demonstration of a triange that you could manipulate. You can drag each point left or right which left representing 0 and right representing 255 on the RGB scale. If you match the background, you win.

Dot Doodle ****NON SMARTBOARD****

This experience is a delightful expression of your imagination. The flow allows for a peaceful experience and the colors calm your mind.

Wave

In this demonstration, you can manipulate the amplitude of the sine, cosine, and tangent waves. This artful display of mathematics is elegant and simple in a way that conveys the wonders of math.

Circles

This is a game where you create bubbles that float around and bounce. When they touch the get frozen in place to create a magical design that is new every time. Click to create bubbles, have fun.

Genetics

In this low tech simultion, you can see how alleles can interact when you have two organisms combining DNA. The bright colors and simplistic shapes create an almost melancholy feel to the aesthetic of this app.
